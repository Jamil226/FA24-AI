Senegalese musician Youssou N'Dour has been named a winner of a BBC Radio 3 World Music Award.The concert will be broadcast on Radio 3's World Music Day the following evening and will feature performances by many of the award winners.It will also include the announcement of the Audience Award, chosen by Radio 3 and BBC World Service listeners, alongside BBC Four viewers.Highlights of the Poll Winners Concert, to be held at The Sage, Gateshead, will be televised on BBC Four on 11 March.
