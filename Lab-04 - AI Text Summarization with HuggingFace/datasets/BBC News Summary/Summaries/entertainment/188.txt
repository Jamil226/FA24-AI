Ice skating duo Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean are to front a similar celebrity ice dance show for ITV, titled Stars on Thin Ice.The celebrities will have to practise a stipulated ice dance and perform it at an ice rink with their partner.The judges will have 50% of the vote to decide who wins the contest, with the ice rink audience making up the rest of the vote.Each celebrity will be paired with a professional skater to impress a panel of judges and win the audience vote.The contestants on Stars on Thin Ice will each be paired up with a professional skater and will learn a new routine every week.
