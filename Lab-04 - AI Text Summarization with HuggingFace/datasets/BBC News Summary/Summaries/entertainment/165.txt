Comedian Chris Morris, who created controversial TV show Brass Eye, is to return to screens with a new sitcom about a spoof London media worker.As well as Brass Eye, Morris was behind another news show satire, The Day Today, and dark sketch comedy Jam.A Brass Eye satire of the media handling of paedophilia sparked 2,500 complaints in 2001.Morris will direct and co-write Nathan Barley - a character from cult website TV Go Home - for Channel 4.
